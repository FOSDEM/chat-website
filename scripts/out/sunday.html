Welcome to FOSDEM 2022!

Welcome to our fully virtual version of the ULB, thanks to the amazing people at Matrix.org! FOSDEM is spread out over a lot of rooms at chat.fosdem.org. Use the webapp for the best experience.

Visit the main conference floor to see all rooms or visit the schedule to find out what's on in each room. Then click on your favourite room, sit back and enjoy the talk.

Confused? Unsure how to proceed? Visit the practical information page, look in the FAQ or talk to a human at the Infodesk!
